AUDIO FILES

To avoid copyright issues, please use royalty-free music in your app. Here are some sources for legal, free-to-use music:

1. Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/music/
   - Completely free for personal and commercial use
   - No attribution required

2. Free Music Archive: https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - Check individual licenses (many are CC BY or CC0)
   - Some require attribution

3. Bensound: https://www.bensound.com/
   - Free for personal use
   - Commercial use requires subscription or one-time purchase

4. ccMixter: http://ccmixter.org/
   - Creative Commons licensed music
   - Check individual track licenses

5. SoundCloud: https://soundcloud.com/
   - Look for tracks with Creative Commons licenses
   - Always verify the specific license terms

How to use:
1. Download an MP3 file from one of these sources
2. Rename it to "placeholder-track.mp3"
3. Place it in this directory (src/assets/audio/)
4. If required by the license, add attribution in your app

IMPORTANT: Always respect copyright laws and license terms when using music in your application.
